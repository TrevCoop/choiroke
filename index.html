Choiroke

on Vancouver Island

Singing together is fun and good for you, but it can also become scary if we get too focused on our performance rather than having fun. Let's try to set aside perfectionism and focus on the fun!

Choiroke is a short, sober, afternoon singalong where we all get a chance to learn the songs in advance. Each song will have a leader, but everyone is encouraged to participate, whether that's through singing (harmonies encouraged), dancing, clapping, or using an instrument. If the leader sings well, cool, but that's definitely not required! This is a supportive atmosphere where we focus more on getting involved.

How it works

If you would like to lead on songs, send us both the karaoke track of the song from YouTube, as well as a link to the original song. We'll pick a set and arrange the songs into a playlist that's roughly one hour long. Then we publish this playlist so we have a chance to listen to the songs in advance. Show up to the event, and when your song(s) come up, get up and lead us in singing!

Date and time

We'll meet roughly every two months on a Sunday afternoon, generally from 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM.
Singing starts at 2:30 sharp, so aim to get there by 2:15. We have a half hour to clean up and wind down.

Location

Cedar Heritage Centre, 1644 MacMillan Rd, Nanaimo, BC V9X 1L9

The venue entrance has a flight of stairs, but it also has a ramp around back and accessible washrooms.
